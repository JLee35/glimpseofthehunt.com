Getting Started


Preserving a dream starts the second the heart stops. Your trophy needs to be brought to the shop or frozen immediately. Remember to keep skin to skin, hair to hair, if frozen, and try to hide the lips, eyes, and ears inside the cape to protect these sensitive areas. My goal is to have your trophy back in six months so you can display your dream! Thanks for your business and good luck hunting!
